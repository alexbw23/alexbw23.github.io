About the sport

Soccer is a world wide sport. Typically, you play with 10 players on the field and a goalkeeper. There are different types of formations, but they are all used for different reasons.

Positions

categories number
goalkeeper 1
defenders 4,5 (centerbacks) and 2,3 (outside backs/full backs)
midfielders 6,8 and 10 (center midfielders)
forwards 7,11 (outside forwards/wingers) and 9 (center forward)
